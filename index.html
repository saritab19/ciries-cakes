About Us

Cirie's Cakes grew from Elva's passion for baking and her joy in sharing the flavors she grew up with. What began as homemade treats for friends quickly turned into a heartfelt way to be part of birthdays, gatherings, and celebrations across the city.

Whether it's Tres Leches, Brazo de Reina, or a custom-made cake, every dessert is handcrafted with patience, love, and the comforting flavor of something baked right at home.
